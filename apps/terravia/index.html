The problem we solve

Land is not undervalued.
It is misunderstood.

Where planning outcomes are unclear, buyers become nervous, transactions slow down, and negotiating leverage shifts toward experienced buyers willing to absorb risk.

Owners struggle to realise full potential value — not because the land is weak, but because the planning position has never been properly assessed and communicated.

TerraVia investigates the real conditions, identifies constraints early, explains the commercial implications, and recommends the most suitable route forward.

How we work

There is no single route

The right path depends on planning complexity, seller appetite, timeline pressures, likely planning outcomes, buyer demand, and risk profile. We determine the most intelligent route for that specific opportunity.

01
Route 1
Planning & Harvest Partnership
Collaborative planning and value enhancement. We fund and coordinate surveys, planning analysis, and architectural studies upfront to bring land to market in its strongest possible position.
PIP strategy Licensing Upfront funding
02
Route 2
Conditional Acquisition
A direct offer subject to planning approval. Provides greater certainty and a cleaner transaction structure. TerraVia assumes the planning risk directly, reducing the owner's involvement.
Certainty Clean structure Risk transfer
03
Route 3
Open Market or Legalisation
Sometimes the most intelligent outcome is a conventional sale, a legalisation strategy, or a direct buyer introduction. The philosophy is flexibility, not formula.

How assessment works

Six stages.
One clear outcome.

We do not start with a solution. We start by listening. Every decision — which consultants, which application type, which timing — follows from genuine understanding of the site, the owner's objectives, and the real planning conditions.

01
Listen
Understand ownership structure, objectives, risk appetite, desired outcome, and timescale constraints.
02
Investigate
Review PDM, urban/rustic status, ICNF, heritage, topography, access, utilities, and precedent.
03
Strategise
Compare possible routes. Recommend the strongest planning and commercial path with clear reasoning.
04
Assemble
Coordinate architects, surveyors, engineers, environmental consultants, and commercial advisors.
05
Submit
Prepare and manage applications, Câmara meetings, authority communication, and revisions.
06
Resolve
Guide through planning outcome, sale, development, holding strategy, or partnership decision.
